EnOcean

Pairing a sensor

  1. If your device has a "Teach-in" button, press it. If your device has no Teach-in button (such as PTM-based device), make your device send a regular telegram.
  2. After a little while, your EnOcean device should appear in the "Discovered items" list.
  3. Then, click on the item you wish to add in your sensor network and give it a name and a location using the user interface that will appear in this right column.
  4. Then, click on the "Add Item" button. You Should see an positive confirmation feedback.

Adding a logical actuator

Use the Manual Adding panel located in the panel below
  1. Specify a name for your actuator.
  2. Specify a location for your actuator.
  3. Specify the profile of your actuator.
  4. Then, click on the "Add Item" button.
